LeapFrog Challenge.

LeapFrog Challenge is a fun, energetic game where players leap like frogs to race to the finish line. With rules that focus on fair play and frog-like movement, it's a perfect mix of fitness and fun. Great for team spirit, coordination, and laughter!

  • Frog Jumps Only â€“ Players must squat and leap forward like a frog. No running or walking.
  • Hands Down Before Jump â€“ Both hands must touch the ground before each jump.
  • Stay in Your Lane â€“ No blocking or touching others. Stay in your own path.
  • First to Finish Wins â€“ Cross the finish line with a proper frog jump to win.

Short game 2

Rooster Run â€“ Game Rules
  • Players must hop forward while holding one leg with one arm, maintaining a cockfighting stance. Running or walking is not allowed.
  • All players start at the starting line in the rooster posture.
  • Players are not allowed to touch or push othersâ€”no hands, no collisions.
  • A player will be disqualified if they:
    • Put their other leg down
    • Release the leg they are holding
    • Pushes or touches another player
  • Each player must carry a baton while racing. Dropping the baton will result in disqualification.
  • The first player to cross the finish line while maintaining the proper posture and holding the baton wins.

Short game 3

The Blind Seeker
  • One player is blindfolded and starts inside the play area (board).
  • Another player stands outside the boundary and acts as the guide.
  • The blindfolded player must hold on to a given object (e.g., a rope, stick, or ball) and a baton throughout the game.
  • The guide gives verbal directions and guides the blindfolded player all the way to the finish line.
  • At the finish line, the blindfolded player must drop the object and the baton inside a basket placed at the end of the line.
  • The blindfolded player must not remove the blindfold, let go of the object, or drop the baton before reaching the basket. If any of these happen, they must restart.
  • If the blindfolded player touches the border line, they must restart.
  • The blindfolded player need to successfully drop the object inside the basket and pass the baton to the next teammate.

Short game 4

Sack Race
  • Each team has players waiting in line with one sack and one baton.
  • Only when a player receives the baton, they may get into the sack and start moving forward.
  • Players can hop, walk, or run â€” but they must stay inside the sack at all times.
  • Each player must carry the baton while racing. If the baton drops he needs to pick it up and move forward without stepping out of the sack
  • If a player falls, they must get back up without stepping out of the sack.
  • After reaching the designated point, the baton is passed to the next teammate.
